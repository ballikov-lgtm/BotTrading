How the Strategy Works

Multi-timeframe confluence — daily trend structure meets 15-minute precision entries.

Step 01

Daily Trend Detection

The bot reads the daily chart and identifies bull or bear structure using swing highs and lows. Neutral markets are skipped entirely.

Step 02

15-Minute Entry Signal

Once the daily trend is confirmed, the bot waits for a pullback on the 15m chart and a clean breakout of a swing level to trigger entry.

Step 03

ATR-Buffered Stop Loss

Stop is placed below the swing reference with an ATR × 0.5 buffer. Minimum 0.3% distance enforced — no unrealistically tight stops.

Step 04

Three Take-Profit Levels

Position split across three targets using EMA levels, swing structure and Fibonacci extensions. Stop trails to break-even at TP1.

Step 05

Risk Management

Fixed 1% portfolio risk per trade. Position size calculated automatically. 5% daily drawdown circuit-breaker halts trading if the session turns bad.

Step 06

AI Research Layer

Twice-daily market research scan adds a sentiment filter. Economic event blackout blocks entries ±60 min around FOMC, NFP and CPI releases.

Three Take-Profit System

Once TP1 is hit the trade cannot lose money — stop moves to break-even automatically.

LevelPosition SizeTargetSL Move AfterOutcome If Stopped
TP1 40% Nearest EMA / swing / Fib level SL → Break-even Break-even (profitable)
TP2 35% Second structural level SL → TP1 (lock profit) Partial win
TP3 25% Third structural level Full exit Full win

Built-In Safety Rules

Six layers of protection that automatically adapt to market conditions.

🧭

HTF Neutral Filter

No clear daily trend = no trade. The bot never guesses in a choppy market.

📰

Research Conflict Filter

If AI sentiment contradicts the technical entry direction, the trade is skipped.

⚠️

Economic Event Blackout

All entries halted ±60 minutes around FOMC, NFP, CPI and major Fed events.

⏳

3-Hour Symbol Cooldown

After a clean stop-out, that specific token is blocked for 3 hours. No re-entering the same failed setup.

📊

Daily Trade Limit

Maximum 3 entries per symbol per day regardless of how many signals fire.

🛑

Daily Drawdown Limit

If today's losses exceed 5% of portfolio, all trading stops until tomorrow. Hard circuit-breaker.
